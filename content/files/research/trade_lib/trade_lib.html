Instruction for GAMS program.

"gams_20061113.zip" includes GAMS codes for implementing the simulation in the above paper (the model part). To use these codes, you must have the full license of GTAP 6 data and GAMS solver PATH. Extract the zip archive and read README.txt file. If you have any question about the program, email me.
